About Me

    Thanks for coming to my corner of the internet!

    I grew up in Pakistan where I did my undergrad in EE at LUMS. After finishing my undergrad in 2012, I came to Michigan for grad school, where I got a masters in EE. In 2014, I moved to Boston for work and have been in the area ever since.

    Here in the Boston area, I work at MathWorks as a software engineer on the compilers team, specifically improving the Simulink Coder and Embedded Coder products.

    I like working on challenging problems in the compilers and backend systems area that involve infrastructure, performance, and scalability.

About Me

    Thanks for coming to my corner of the internet!

    I grew up in Pakistan where I did my undergrad in EE at LUMS. After finishing my undergrad in 2012, I came to Michigan for grad school, where I got a masters in EE. In 2014, I moved to Boston and worked for MathWorks in their compilers team, specifically developing features for the Simulink Coder and Embedded Coder products.

    In 2018, I joined LinkedIn in the SF Bay Area. Here, I work on the backend web services for the LinkedIn feed.
